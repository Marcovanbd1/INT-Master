For the 30 day sprint, the cost is intentionally boring. We fund two things only, physical listening points, and stewardship time.

30 day sprint, indicative range

£1,500 to £2,000 all in, depending on disc volume and cadence.

What this covers

1) Physical listening layer

Microsuction discs and entry points in everyday spaces, kitchens, mirrors, noticeboards, lanyards, libraries, cafés, community venues. These are not promotional, they are neighbourhood listening infrastructure.

2) Stewardship

Weekly interpretation, light coordination, and a short public-facing “what we heard and how we responded” brief. This keeps the loop closed and prevents drift into inert insight.

No licences. No procurement dependency. No integration work. Just a timeboxed practice of safe listening and gentle response.

What this does not cover

Clinical triage, case management, targeted campaigns, individual tracking, or anything that requires personal data.

If you need a line-item breakdown for an internal paper, keep it simple: discs, and stewardship time.
